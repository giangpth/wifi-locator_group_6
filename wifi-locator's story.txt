Wifi-locator's Story:
1, WiList: List all wifi appeared around, each wifi as an entry that contain fields: SSID, strength of wifi.
2, History: List of wifi have connected.
3, Sort: Arrange list of wifi by optional key.
4, Rate: Rate the wifi for later time.
5, Find location: Find location of the device basing on the wifi signals.
6, display the information of curent location.
7, allow user to add information for their locator.
8, Save the recent location.
